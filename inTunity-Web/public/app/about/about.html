About INTUNITY

Life is a movie. We are all actors. INTUNITY is the soundtrack
Our mission at INTUNITY is to serve each user with a music stream curated and vetted by those he or she trusts the mosts.

Mining for jewels has always been a team effort
We aspire to provide each user with the soundtrack to aspects of his life that he shares with his connections within the INTUNITY community, rather than provide music representative of tastes by geographic region.

A picture is worth a thousand words. A song can speak for 1000 pictures
Here at INTUNITY, we believe that a platform for convergence via music should accept only what each individual user deems his highest quality contributions to the community. We strive to promote such contribution through a working philosophy of minimalism that permeates through both our team and the product itself.
